getBoxlock.com

Boxlock is a wifi-enabled package protection device that safeguards multiple home deliveries. Developed and prototyped at propelland, the Boxlock launched on Kickstarter in November 2017. It is currently at over 200% of the $20,000 goal.

I designed one of the final iterations and oversaw the prototype assembly.
